Q & S was founded on March 15th, 2020 in response to the COVID-19 pandemic. Several of our members were isolating in place, and needed a meeting to go to. We meet every day at 7:30am and Mon, Tue, Wed, Fri and Sun at 7pm ET *. The full schedule along with links to join via Zoom or Phone is listed on the Atlanta-Area Intergroup Website.

* On Thu and Sat evenings, many members go to the 5th Tradition Group. They offer a Closed Big Book Study on Thu @ 8pm and an Open Speaker Meeting on Sat @ 7pm.
